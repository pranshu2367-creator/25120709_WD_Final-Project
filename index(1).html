Our Story

Where Tradition
Meets Style

Blade & Co. was founded by Aaron Joseph in 2024, right here in Pune. What started as a passion project while studying at FLAME University has grown into a go-to grooming destination for clients who care about quality. We believe a great haircut isn't just about looks — it's about confidence, ritual, and the time you take for yourself.
